WHY CHOOSE US?

Our Specialities

Situated at the very heart of Rhodes town, the hotel provides easy access to all major points of interest all within walking distance. The hotel is renowned in the local market due to its operation which “runs” 365 days a year. All rooms have been renovated recently and offer a unique design with modern furnishes. Being in constant operation for the last 47 years, and gone through numerous renovations the hotel operates mainly with repeaters who value and appreciate its comfort, cleanliness, family attitude, amenities and personality just to name a few. On line booking is available through mainstream agencies or directly with the hotel, with its own platform at www.lydiahotel.com were various offers are available all through the year.
Our reception operates 24 hours and 7 days a week, making check in or providing information a breeze. All the information one might need for sightseeing the island either by boat, bike, car or motorbike is available there. Tickets for shows, boat trips or museums are also available, as well as car hire from the hotels own rental company, offering lower rates than any competitor local or otherwise.
